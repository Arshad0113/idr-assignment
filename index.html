Who We Are

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to closing the talent gap in digital, cyber and technology risk.

Backed by leading UK university partnerships, we bridge academic insight with real-world execution across regulated and high-consequence environments.
