# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Assignment

## 1. Logo Design

Two variants are included:

- Icon-only cube logo
- Icon + text "Institute of Digital Risk"

The cube represents structure, resilience and layered risk architecture in digital systems.  
The internal grid lines symbolize interconnected networks and risk layers managed by digital risk professionals.

The color palette uses **orange, black and white** to represent innovation, security and professionalism.
